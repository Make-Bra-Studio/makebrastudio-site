Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 8, 2026

This privacy policy explains how Roman Chernukha, doing business as Make Bra Studio ("we," "us," or "our"), handles information in connection with the following mobile applications:

Junkyard Master Amaze Island Puzzle 2020: Apocalypse Puzzle Block

All of our games are free to download and are supported by advertising and optional in-app purchases. By using any of our games, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect any personal information from you. Our games do not require accounts, registration, or login. However, our games use third-party services that may automatically collect certain data on our behalf.

2. Third-Party Services

Our games use the following third-party services, each of which may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies:

Unity LevelPlay — ad mediation platform that manages which ad networks serve ads in our games. It may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to optimize ad delivery. Learn more: Unity Privacy Policy

Ad Networks. The following ad networks may serve advertisements in our games through LevelPlay. Each may collect device identifiers, IP address, ad interaction data, and usage data:

  • Google AdMob — Google anonymizes IP data after 9 months and cookie data after 18 months. AdMob Policy
  • Unity Ads — Unity Privacy Policy
  • ironSource Ads — ironSource Privacy Policy
  • AppLovin — may be added in the future. AppLovin Privacy Policy

When active, we work with AppLovin to deliver ads in our mobile applications and other devices and/or platforms. For more information about how AppLovin uses your data, please visit their Privacy Policy.

Unity Analytics — collects usage and gameplay data such as session length, device information, and in-game events to help us understand how our games are used. Learn more: Unity Privacy Policy

Google Play Services — provides core Android functionality. May collect device information, log data, unique application numbers, local storage, and cookies. Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

3. How Information Is Used

The data collected by third-party services is used to:

  • Display relevant advertisements and optimize ad delivery across networks
  • Measure ad performance
  • Analyze gameplay behavior, session data, and in-game events to improve our games
  • Maintain and improve service functionality
  • Detect fraud and abuse

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only in the following cases:

  • With third-party service providers listed above, as necessary to operate our games
  • When required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or legal process
  • With your prior consent

5. Children's Privacy

Our games are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information through our games, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us at the email below.

European Economic Area and United Kingdom (GDPR / UK GDPR). If you are located in the EEA or UK, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data, as well as the right to restrict or object to processing. Since we do not directly collect personal data, most data subject requests should be directed to the relevant third-party provider (see Section 2 above). You may also contact us and we will assist where possible.

California (CCPA). If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, request its deletion, and opt out of its sale. We do not sell personal information. To exercise any rights, contact us at the email below.

Opting out of personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:
